## Dev Notes

### Technical Implementation
- **ASO Strategy:** Keyword optimization for marine, boating, NMEA, autopilot, and sailing terms
- **Content Creation:** Professional video and screenshot production with marine environment focus
- **Website:** SEO-optimized landing pages with conversion tracking
- **Community Engagement:** Targeted outreach to boating enthusiasts and marine communities

### Marketing Strategy
- **Target Audience:** Serious recreational sailors and powerboaters with autopilot systems
- **Value Proposition:** First mobile autopilot control app with professional marine reliability
- **Differentiation:** Comprehensive autopilot control vs. display-only competitors
- **Pricing:** Premium positioning ($79.99) justified by unique autopilot capability

### Content Requirements
- **Screenshots:** Show real autopilot control, NMEA data, professional marine environment
- **Videos:** Demonstrate actual autopilot engagement and control on real boats
- **Testimonials:** Focus on safety, convenience, and professional marine use cases
- **Documentation:** Technical accuracy for marine electronics installers
