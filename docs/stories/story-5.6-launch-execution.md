## Dev Notes

### Technical Implementation
- **Coordination:** Multi-platform simultaneous release execution with automated deployment
- **Monitoring:** Real-time visibility into launch metrics, user acquisition, and technical issues
- **Response:** Rapid issue resolution capability during high-visibility launch period

### Launch Strategy
- **Target Market:** Serious recreational boaters with autopilot systems and budget for premium marine apps
- **Value Proposition:** First comprehensive mobile autopilot control solution with professional marine reliability
- **Pricing:** Premium positioning justified by unique autopilot capability and safety features
- **Geography:** Initial launch in English-speaking markets (US, UK, Australia, Canada)

### Success Metrics
- **Downloads:** 500 downloads in first week, 1000 in first month
- **Revenue:** $12,000 target by month 12 based on pricing and conversion
- **Ratings:** Maintain 4.0+ star average across all app stores
- **Technical:** 99.5% crash-free sessions during launch period
