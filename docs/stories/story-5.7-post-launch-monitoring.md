## Story

**As a** product manager managing post-launch operations  
**I want** comprehensive monitoring and feedback systems  
**So that** we can optimize the product and plan future development

4. App store rating and review management

### Feedback Integration
6. User feedback collection and analysis system
7. Feature request prioritization framework
8. Customer advisory board establishment
9. Beta user community transition to power user advocates
10. Community forum moderation and engagement

### Future Planning
11. Product roadmap planning based on user feedback
12. Next version feature prioritization
13. Platform expansion opportunities assessment
14. Market expansion strategy development
15. Revenue and user growth tracking against projections
